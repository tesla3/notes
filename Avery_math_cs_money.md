# Avery — Math, CS & Summer Planning (Rising 9th Grade)

**Date:** 2026-05-16

---

## Profile

- Rising 9th grader (currently 8th grade)
- Says she likes math but fears she's "not as good"
- Less confident, self-limiting
- Current framing: "CS pays well" — optimizing for monetary return
- The money frame likely doubles as an emotional shield (safer than "I love math but what if I fail")

---

## Key Insights

1. **"Not as good" is a comparison problem** — likely measuring herself against the loudest/fastest kid, not against actual ability. Speed ≠ depth.

2. **"CS pays well" is anxiety, not passion** — she wants safety and control. Valid instinct, but wrong compass for a 14-year-old.

3. **The highest-paying CS jobs ARE math jobs** — ML, quant, crypto, research. Math is the ceiling; code is the floor.

4. **The confidence gap and the money frame are connected** — both avoid vulnerability. "I'm being practical" is safer than "I love this."

---

## Strategy: Side-Door Approach

Don't fight the money frame. Use it as a Trojan horse for math.

### Reframe (say to her):
> "The people who get paid the MOST in CS aren't the best coders. They're the best mathematical thinkers who also code. Math IS the money move. Code is just the delivery mechanism."

### Do NOT:
- ❌ Say "you're so smart, you can do it" (she has counter-evidence)
- ❌ Sign her up for an intensive competition camp (confirms fear)
- ❌ Frame anything as remediation
- ❌ Pressure — if she says "not yet," accept it. The seed is planted.

---

## Summer Plan (3 Phases)

### June — Rebuild "I figure things out" identity
- Low-stakes, creative, feels like CS:
  - Build a stock/crypto price predictor (Python + pandas) — it's actually statistics
  - Project Euler problems (math puzzles disguised as code)
  - Generative art with p5.js (geometry + algebra)

### July — Structured math in safe environment
- **AoPS online course** (Intro to Counting & Probability or Number Theory) — self-paced, pseudonymous, struggle is normalized
- OR **Girls' Angle** / local math circle (girls-only, exploration over ranking)
- OR **3Blue1Brown neural network series → implement from scratch** ("I built a neural network" = social currency, substrate is pure math)

### August — Agency
- Let her choose: go deeper or move on
- If she chooses deeper → the seed sprouted
- If not → it's still planted
