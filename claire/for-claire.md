# ML Ideas for Break

Some things you could explore if you're interested.

- **Your robot's data** — Your team measured power at 13 distances. Can you predict the right power for distances you never tested? Fit curves, figure out when a model is memorizing vs actually learning, pick the best one.

- **Build a neural net from scratch** — A tiny engine, ~120 lines of Python, no libraries. Every gradient is the chain rule from calc. Train it on your robot data, then try images — handwritten digits, maybe clothing. See how far you want to take it.

- **Run an LLM on our GPU** — Ask it questions from subjects you actually know — calc, chem, FTC rules. Grade its answers. Figure out *why* it's bad at some things and not others.

- **Break an image classifier** — Use the same math that trains one to trick it. Add a tiny invisible change to a photo and make a banana look like a toaster.

- **Train a classifier for FTC** — Take photos of game elements, train a model on them, find out where it works and where it falls apart.
