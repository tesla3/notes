**Critical Finding:** The summer camp is part of a massive **Chinese government cultural influence operation**, not a private cultural exchange.

## Key Discovery: Chinese Bridge Program Connection

### The Pattern

2. **全美华裔青少年协会** (American Chinese Youth Association) - same programs, same cities
3. **Mandarin Playground** - same format, same funding structure
4. **Multiple other organizations** - coordinated recruitment across different US markets

### The Chinese Government Connection

**Center for Language Education and Cooperation (CLEC)**
- **Official Ministry of Education agency** - not private organization
- **Replaced Hanban/Confucius Institute Headquarters** in 2020 rebranding  
- **Manages all "Chinese Bridge" summer camp programs globally**

### Evidence Sources

- Donghua University Shanghai: "Sponsored by the Ministry of Education's CLEC"

- Yunnan University: "Supported by CLEC of the Ministry of Education"
