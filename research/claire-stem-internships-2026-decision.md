## Key Insight

The original research framed this as a crisis of missed deadlines. That's half-right. The deeper issue is that Claire has **no research experience** — which is the single thing elite programs filter on. A fancy summer program helps, but the real 2027 strategy is:

1. **Get any research experience this summer** (SRTI > Cooper Union for this purpose)
2. **Start UCSD MAP in October** (8 months of faculty mentorship)
3. **Enter science fairs in junior year** (competitions = credentials)
4. **Apply to 2027 elite programs in Oct–Dec 2026** with a completely different application profile

The summer 2026 program isn't the destination. It's the first credential in a 12-month build toward competitive 2027 applications.
