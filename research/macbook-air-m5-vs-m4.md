# MacBook Air M5 vs M4: Worth the Upgrade?

**Date:** 2026-03-04 (M5 announced 2026-03-03, pre-orders open today)

## Verdict

**If you already have an M4 Air: No.** The M5 Air is a mild spec bump, not a generational leap. Same design, same display, same battery life, $100 more.

**If you're on M1/M2 or older: Get the M5.** The base model now starts at 512GB storage (was 256GB on M4), and the cumulative chip gains from M1→M5 are substantial. The 16/512 config is actually **$100 cheaper** than the equivalent M4 16/512 was.

**If deciding between discounted M4 vs new M5 at similar price: Get the M5.** You get newer silicon, Wi-Fi 7/BT 6 (N1 chip), faster SSD, and longer software support runway.

## Key Differences

| Spec | M5 Air | M4 Air |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Price (13")** | $1,099 | $999 |
| **Price (15")** | $1,299 | $1,199 |
| **Base storage** | 512GB | 256GB |
| **CPU** | 10-core (base 8-core, upgrade to 10) | 10-core |
| **Memory bandwidth** | 153 GB/s (+28%) | 120 GB/s |
| **Connectivity** | Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6 (N1 chip) | Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3 |
| **SSD speed** | ~2x faster read/write | Standard |
| **Display** | Liquid Retina LED, 60Hz | Liquid Retina LED, 60Hz |
| **Battery** | 18h video / 15h web | 18h video / 15h web |
| **Design** | Identical | Identical |
| **Ports** | 2x Thunderbolt 4 + headphone | 2x Thunderbolt 4 + headphone |

## Performance (Apple's claims)

- **AI tasks:** 4x faster than M4, 9.5x faster than M1
- **3D rendering (Blender ray-tracing):** 1.5x faster than M4, 6.5x faster than M1
- **Video enhancement (Topaz):** 1.9x faster than M4
- **Image processing (Affinity):** 1.5x faster than M4

Geekbench 6 (M5 Pro, not Air — Air will throttle lower due to fanless design):
- Single-core: ~4,288 (M4 Air: 3,751)
- Multi-core: ~17,926 (M4 Air: 14,947)

**Caveat:** Air is fanless → will throttle under sustained heavy loads. The M5 Air won't match M5 Pro sustained performance. For casual/productivity use, difference vs M4 is imperceptible.

## What Didn't Change (Disappointments)

- **Still 60Hz display** — no ProMotion/120Hz. iPhones have had 120Hz since 2021.
- **Still LED, not OLED or mini-LED** — rumored for Pros at M6, Airs maybe later.
- **Same port situation** — still only 2 Thunderbolt + headphone jack.
- **Same design** — 3rd year of this chassis (M3, M4, M5).
- **Same battery life** — no improvement claimed.
- **$100 price increase** — partially offset by doubled base storage.

## Pricing Nuance

The base price went up $100, but Apple doubled the base storage from 256GB→512GB. So:
- **16/256 is gone** — you can't buy the cheapest config anymore
- **16/512 is $100 cheaper** than the equivalent M4 16/512 was
- **Gotcha:** Base M5 has 8 GPU cores; M4's 16/512 came with 10 GPU cores. Upgrading M5 to 10-core GPU costs more.

In some markets (EU, Canada), the 24/512 M5 config is actually cheaper than the M4 equivalent was.

## Who Should Buy What

| You are... | Recommendation |
|------------|---------------|
| M4 Air owner | **Skip.** Wait for OLED/ProMotion (rumored ~2028-2029 for Air) |
| M3 Air owner | **Probably skip** unless you need Wi-Fi 7 or more AI perf |
| M1/M2 Air owner | **Good upgrade.** Meaningful gains across the board |
| Intel Mac owner | **Buy immediately.** Any Apple Silicon is a massive leap |
| Developer (compile times matter) | **M5 worth it** for the single-core improvement |
| Content creator (video/3D) | **M5 worth it** for GPU + media engine gains |
| Student/office worker | **Get discounted M4** — difference is imperceptible for productivity |
| "Buy and hold" 4+ years | **M5** for longer software support runway |

## Community Sentiment

Reddit/forum consensus is overwhelmingly: "if you need it now, buy it now." The M4→M5 gap is the smallest generational jump yet. Most comments recommend buying whichever is cheaper at the time of purchase. Several users note their M1 still works perfectly fine for casual use.
