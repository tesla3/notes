## Updated Recommendations

### **Consider ElitePrep San Diego IF**:
- Budget allows $15,000-25,000 for college consulting
- Want structured, multi-year planning starting 9th/10th grade  
- Value dedicated local team with recent Ivy League results
- Comfortable with pricing discussion during consultation

### **Choose Alternatives IF**:
- Seeking transparent pricing and value proposition
- Want college consulting specialist (vs. test prep + consulting)
- Budget-conscious ($3,000-8,000 range)  
- Prefer direct consultant accountability over franchise model

### **Recommended Due Diligence**:
1. **Request specific pricing** before any consultation
2. **Ask for recent client references** (not just testimonials)
3. **Compare per-session rates** to independent consultants  
4. **Verify staff credentials** and college counseling experience
5. **Understand refund/cancellation policies** before signing

## Conclusion

The **San Diego Pacific Highlands location appears to be a legitimate, well-staffed operation** with recent strong outcomes. However, **pricing opacity and premium costs** make it difficult to recommend over transparent alternatives unless budget is not a constraint.

**Key Decision Point**: ElitePrep SD offers proven results but at likely 2-3x the cost of equally qualified independent consultants. The question becomes whether the structured approach and dedicated team justify the premium pricing.

**Bottom Line**: *Not the scam initially suspected, but expensive relative to value delivered. Good option for families with substantial budgets who want structured, multi-year planning - but investigate pricing thoroughly before committing.*
