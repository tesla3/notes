### Faculty Cold Outreach (the hidden option)

The review file flags this as "often the most effective route for late applicants." Many UCSD, SDSU, and USD professors will take a motivated high school student who shows up with Python skills and asks to help with computational work. This is especially viable because:

- Claire's strength is computation, not wet lab — professors need Python help year-round
- No formal application process = no deadline
- Can start anytime, flexible hours
- Produces the same credential (research experience, potential poster/paper) as formal programs
- Works even if every formal program falls through

**This is the safety net that never expires.**
