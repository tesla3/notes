# AP Exam Difficulty Rankings (2025 Data)

Source: College Board 2025 score distributions, various aggregators
Date: 2025-05-10

## Hardest to Score a 5

| Exam | % earning a 5 | % earning 3+ (pass) | Notes |
|------|---------------|---------------------|-------|
| AP English Literature | ~7.4% | ~43.9% (lowest of all APs) | Hardest by both metrics |
| AP US History | ~14% | ~74% | Huge content scope; DBQ trips people up |
| AP Chemistry | ~17–18% | ~78% | Highest pass rate of any science AP, but hard to ace |
| AP Physics 1 | ~19.8% | moderate | ~19% also score a 1 (bimodal) |
| AP Physics C: E&M | ~23% | — | Dropped from 35% in 2024 after exam revision |

## Hardest by Pass Rate (% scoring 3+)

1. AP English Literature — ~43.9%
2. AP Physics 1
3. AP Environmental Science
4. AP English Language & Composition

## Easiest to Score a 5 (self-selection caveat)

- AP Chinese Language & Culture — ~60%+ (many heritage speakers)
- AP Calculus BC — strong STEM self-selection
- AP Physics C: Mechanics — self-selected population

## Key Insights

- **Pass rate ≠ difficulty to get a 5.** AP Chemistry has a 78% pass rate but only 17% score a 5. AP English Lit is hard on both ends.
- **Self-selection distorts.** AP Chinese's 89% pass rate reflects heritage speakers, not exam ease.
- **AP Physics C: E&M** saw a dramatic drop in 5-rate (35% → 23%) after 2025 exam revisions.
- **AP Physics 1** is nearly bimodal: ~20% score a 5, ~19% score a 1.

## AP Chemistry Specifics

- Single exam offered (no A/B split anymore)
- Format: 60 MCQ (no calculator) + 7 FRQ (calculator allowed, 1hr 45min)
- 9 units; equilibrium, acids/bases, intermolecular forces dominate FRQs
- 2026 exam window: May 4–15
