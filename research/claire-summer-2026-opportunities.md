## Critical Insights

### What Actually Exists vs. Myths

**MYTH**: "High school internships at Google/Microsoft"
**REALITY**: These are 1-2 week exposure workshops, not internships

**MYTH**: "Elite programs are the only valuable ones"
**REALITY**: Regional research programs often provide better mentorship and real outcomes

**MYTH**: "You need research experience to get research experience"
**REALITY**: Many programs specifically target beginners (but require academic strength)
