# Apple Employee Discount Program

Source: Glassdoor, levels.fyi employee self-reports; web search 2026-05-09

## Hardware Discounts

- **25% off** one Mac, iPad, or iPod per year (annual discount)
- **15% off** additional purchases throughout the year
- Applies to iPhones and Apple Watch as well, though exact percentages may vary by category

## Software & Services

- **50% off** most Apple software
- **25% off** AppleCare protection plans

## Friends & Family

- Employees get a limited number of friends & family discount codes (~15% off), typically a few per year

## Other Perks

- Employee stock purchase plan (ESPP) at a discount
- Free/discounted Apple products for onboarding in some roles

## Confidence & Caveats

- All figures from self-reported employee data, not official Apple documentation
- Apple treats internal EPP specifics as confidential
- The 25%/15% structure has been consistently reported for several years but could change without notice
- Regional variation likely exists outside the US
- Quantity caps per product category per year exist but aren't consistently reported
